Services

Our Tire Services

Flat Tire Repair

Puncture plug, patch repair, or full replacement. We assess the damage and give you the honest recommendation. Available 24/7, dispatched fast.

Tire Installation

New tires mounted, balanced, and inflated to spec. Wheel torqued to manufacturer standards. Works for all passenger vehicles, trucks, and vans.

Seasonal Tire Swap

Summer to winter, winter to summer — we handle it all at your driveway. We bring the equipment, you just hand us the keys.

Tire Rotation

Extend the life of your tires with scheduled rotation. We move tires to balance wear patterns and inspect tread depth at no extra charge.

TPMS Service

Tire Pressure Monitoring System sensor reset, replacement, and programming after every tire service. We keep your dashboard warning lights off.

Tire Inspection

Safety check on all four tires — tread depth measurement, sidewall inspection, pressure check, and written report. Peace of mind before any road trip.

Conventional Oil Change

Standard mineral oil change with new OEM-compatible filter. Best for older vehicles or those not requiring synthetic. Includes fluid top-ups and visual inspection.

Drain & refill conventional oil
New oil filter installed
Washer fluid, coolant, brake fluid top-up
Tire pressure check
Multi-point visual inspection

Synthetic Oil Change

Full synthetic oil for maximum engine protection. Required for most modern vehicles, turbocharged engines, and performance cars. Longer intervals between changes.

Premium filter installed

OBD2 reset if applicable
Oil life monitor reset
